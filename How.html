1- POINT your mouse cursor at an emoticon to SELECT IT (a blue box will appear).
2- RIGHT CLICK on the blue box with your mouse and select COPY.
3- POINT to the location that you want the emoticon to appear.
4- RIGHT CLICK on your mouse and select PASTE.
5- Finally, click the "Post to Facebook" button and you're done!

Clicking the "Post To Facebook" image opens your News Feed in a pop-out window so you can post a status update.

Use the Text Box as a staging area for multiple emoticons or as a canvas for your "art work".... Remember to hit "ENTER" on your keyboard after each line so that it will post to Facebook the same way. ... A single period ' . ' followed by the "enter" key on the first line of your post will fix the issue of your name messing up your comment where "return" (enter) posts the comment instead of bumping the cursor to the next line.

✘

Emoticons that consist of muliple peices, like :-) or <3 need a space on each side to work.

✘
